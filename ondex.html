FAQ

Clear answers. No confusion.

Do guests need an app?

No. Guests open a link, RSVP in seconds, and receive updates via WhatsApp or email.

How do reminders work?

Reminders are behavior based. If someone did not open, they get a gentle nudge. If they opened but did not respond, they get a follow up. Confirmed guests get a day-of reminder.

Can I control +1 and guest categories?

Yes. You can allow +1, restrict it, and tag guests as VIP, family, tables, or any category you want.

Is this for weddings only?

No. DearGuest works for weddings, birthdays, corporate events, private parties, and nightlife guestlists.

Can I export the guestlist for the door?

Yes. Export or use a door view with fast search and clear statuses.

What should I build first as an MVP?

Invitation link, RSVP collection, duplicate prevention, and a simple reminders engine. Insights can start with opened and pending.
